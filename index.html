The Lamborghini Aventador (Spanish pronunciation: [aβentaˈðoɾ]) is a mid-engine sports car produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini. The Aventador’s namesake is a Spanish fighting bull that fought in Zaragoza, Aragón, in 1993.[10][11][12] The Aventador is the successor to the Murciélago and was produced in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy.
